# SEO Checklist For BK Parfume

## 1. Google Search Console

- Add property: `https://bkparfume.site/`
- Submit sitemap: `https://bkparfume.site/sitemap.xml`
- Request indexing for:
  - `/`
  - `/zhinochi-parfumy.html`
  - `/cholovichi-parfumy.html`
  - `/uniseks-parfumy.html`
  - `/optova-parfumeriya.html`
  - `/dostavka-i-oplata.html`
- Check Coverage for excluded or duplicate pages.
- Check Performance weekly for queries with high impressions and low CTR.

## 2. Google Business And Brand Signals

- If BK Parfume has a real business location or pickup point, create or update Google Business Profile.
- Keep the same phone and email across the site, directories, and social accounts.
- Add branded descriptions on Instagram, Facebook, and other profiles with a link to the site.

## 3. Commercial Query Expansion

- Track impressions and clicks for these clusters:
  - `наливна парфумерія`
  - `парфуми на розлив`
  - `жіночі парфуми купити`
  - `чоловічі парфуми купити`
  - `унісекс парфуми купити`
  - `оптова парфумерія`
- Expand pages and internal links for queries that start getting impressions.

## 4. Backlinks And Mentions

- Add the site to Ukrainian business directories and catalog listings.
- Secure a few relevant mentions from beauty blogs, gift guides, fragrance communities, or local media.
- Prioritize branded and relevant links over mass low-quality links.

## 5. Reviews And Trust

- Collect customer reviews in text form and publish them on the site later.
- Ask repeat customers for branded mentions in social media.
- Keep delivery, contact, and payment information updated on the site.

## 6. Tracking Cadence

- Review Search Console every 7 days.
- Review index coverage after each batch of new pages.
- Review top landing pages and CTR every 30 days.

## 7. What To Avoid

- Do not buy bulk spam backlinks.
- Do not create dozens of thin doorway pages.
- Do not duplicate the same text across all landing pages.
